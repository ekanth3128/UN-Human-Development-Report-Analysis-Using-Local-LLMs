
Section: Urban Shrinkage of Cities (Chapter 3)

Key Indicators & Analysis: This report identifies significant depopulation trends within cities across Serbia, with the largest city having seen a decline in population by over 15% since its peak. Finanscial stress is evident from increased municipal debt levels and decreasing tax revenues per capita. Urban economic activities have largely shifted to service sectors amidst this shrinking demographic landscape.

Policy Recommendations: To counteract urban decline, the report suggests investing in smart city initiatives with digital technology integration as a long-term solution and enhancing public transportation infrastructure to revitalize local economies by improving accessibility for residents and businesses alike.
