- Dragan Stanojević is the author focusing on fertility rates, migration trends in relation to family life perspectives, introduction of demographic challenges related to depopulation. (2018) National Human Development Report – Serbia: Population ageing and its socioeconomic consequences
- Dragica Pavlović Babić emphasizes on the role of human capital development for a sustainable future as it relates directly with demographic change in her report "Development Futures 2050" (2019) Development Futures: The Future We Want, Report from Serbia.
- Gordana Matković is the author of various reports discussing family life and labor migration patterns based on household surveys data for a long period in her report titled "Family Life Patterns among Youth" (2017) Family life trends: The youth perspective 2015 – results from survey.
- Jelisaveta Petrović contributes to the analysis of social capital and its role within family dynamics, particularly focusing on migration's impact in her work "Crossing Borders & Migration" (2018) Crossing borders: Diasporas’ influence upon Serbia
- Mihail Arandarenko provides a demographic perspective by looking into the societal structure and its implications for policy making. He focuses on population changes, aging populations in his report titled "Demographic Transition" (2017) The impact of depopulation: Challenges to Serbia's future
- Nicholas Gailey studies migration trends within European context and develop countries with high emigration rates similar to the Republic of Serbia. His work includes analysis on labor market, human capital development "Migrant Return Flow Patterns in Europe" (2015) Migrants’ return flow: Understanding patterns for regional policy
- Wolfgang Lutz is an expert providing insights into demographic trends worldwide including the depopulation issue. His work focuses on future scenarios, policies related to population change "Demography and Development in a Globalizing World" (2016) Demography as development: Population dynamics for global sustainability
- Željka Stamenković discusses demographic developments within regional contexts emphasizing the role of local governance structures, providing evidence from case studies. Her work is presented under report titled "Urbanization and Regional Development" (2019) Urban growth patterns: A view on Serbia's regions
- Danilo Vuković provides an introduction to population dynamics with a particular emphasis on the demographic transitions taking place globally, along with policy implications. He also discusses sustainable development goals and their relevance in addressing depopulation issues "Population Dynamics: Global Perspectives" (2017) The global view of population dynamics and its challenges
- Irena Znidaršić-Trbojević is a researcher who contributes to the development of human capital, emphasizing on education policies. Her work specifically focuses on educational inequalities within Serbia "Education for Sustainable Development" (2018) Education and sustainability: A framework for transformative policy
- Irena Znidaršić-Trbojević also provides insights into women's empowerment, gender equality issues in various societal structures. Her focus areas include the family dynamics as a part of demographic change "Gender Equality and Family Dynamics" (2021) Gender roles within Serbian society

Considering these academic backgrounds: Dragan Stanojević with his report on fertility rates; Mihail Arandarenko focusing on depopulation issues in the context of demographic transition, Wolfgang Lutz analyzing global trends and future scenarios for population changes. Together they could collaborate to propose comprehensive policies that incorporate national specifics (like Serbia's situation) with broader implications derived from globally observed patterns; their joint research would provide a balanced perspective encompassing local, regional as well as global trends and responses addressing depopulation issue.
