The current population size of Serbia (6.82 million) would shrink by
1.4 million or by 21% until 2050 according to the baseline scenario. This
puts Serbia in the group of top ten world countries (including its neigh-
bours Croatia, Bulgaria and Romania) expecting to see their popula-

decrease would continue afterwards, though, at a slightly slower pace

the transition in the country’s migration profile from net emigration to
net immigration, and a slight increase in fertility rates.
