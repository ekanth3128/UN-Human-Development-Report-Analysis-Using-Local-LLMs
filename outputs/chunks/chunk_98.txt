• The capacities of preschool institutions cover at least 95% of the
needs of the population in the current year. Of the number of appli-
cations, at least 95% of children are enrolled.

parents — work in the second shift.
• Primary school capacities cover at least 95% of the needs for an ex-
tended stay of children in the current year.

changing and feeding children in public areas and/or in public fa-
cilities

policy.

and children’s nutrition in primary school.

parents.

(parents).
