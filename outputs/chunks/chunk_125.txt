As already mentioned, the consolidation and decrease in teach-
ing staff due to the declining student body is modest and, over the
2017/18 - 2019/20 period, amounts to 400-700 per year, counting all
teachers working in primary schools. The decrease is more discerni-
ble for teachers on full time employment, where the yearly decline is
around 1,000 of them, which is roughly half of the number retiring
yearly. Figure 3 displays the trends regarding all teachers.
