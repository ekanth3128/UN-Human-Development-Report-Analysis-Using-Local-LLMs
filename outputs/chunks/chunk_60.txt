regardless of the region they lived in according to the last available
data at subnational level. Achieved advances in educational attain-
ment of population between 2002 and 2011 has not alleviated regional
differences. The region of the capital (Belgrade) is still distinguished
by the best education structure, with almost 30% of working age peo-
ple having tertiary education (Table 2). That was close to the national
average of the top-ranked countries at the time of the 2011 census.
